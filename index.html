Welcome to TheoryC

"Coding an Augmented Reality Pursuit Rotor for Studying Continuous Skills using the Kinect v2 Sensor"

tl;dr

TheoryC replicates a classic Kinesiology experiment (pursuit rotor task) using a Kinect for Windows sensor to research varying teaching techniques that yield the best performance in acquisition and retention of new skills. The Kinect will collect novel telemetry of human performance to direct future research.

Since this video, I've added additional settings and collecting more data, but it gives you an idea. :)
